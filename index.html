The Project

Using ,

We present PriCipher, a graphical user interface which can predict the prices of the 2 major crops namely: RICE and WHEAT of all the states of the Republic of India.
Since food grains are the most important part of the diet of any household, all efforts must be made to ensure stability of prices and adequate competition, which will ensure prosperity for farmers, consumers and the national economy at large.
Our project aims to rigorously study the factors affecting food grain prices in various parts of the country and to predict with reasonable accuracy the future course which the aforementioned prices will take, and which can be used as a tool by farmers, consumers and policymakers. .

Our project takes in monthly data for the last 5 years for 15 parameters affecting a state's rice & wheat prices:

  1. Population
  2. Percentage of rural and urban population
  3. GDP Per Capita
  4. Rice Rate/Kg
  5. Wheat Rate/Kg.
  6. Average Temperature.
  7. Total Rainfall
  8. WPI of Rice
  9. WPI of Wheat
  10. Diesel Prices
  11. ₹ vs $
  12. Staple Crop Preference
  13. Rice Pref Percent
  14. Wheat Pref Percent
  15. Natural Disaster
